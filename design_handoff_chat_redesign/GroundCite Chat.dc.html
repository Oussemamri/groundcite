What does §25.1309(b) require for catastrophic failure conditions?
retrieving / reranking / generating · 3.2s
GROUNDED0.8412

Under §25.1309(b), airplane systems and associated components, considered separately and in relation to other systems, must be designed so that the occurrence of any failure condition which would prevent the continued safe flight and landing of the airplane is extremely improbable[1].

Any other failure condition which would reduce the capability of the airplane or the ability of the crew to cope with adverse operating conditions must be improbable[2]. Compliance must be shown by analysis and, where necessary, by appropriate ground, flight, or simulator tests[3].
